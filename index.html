Taking the Frustration Out of Container Management

Ease of Use

Fetch, extract, and start a cage (container) with one simple command. Updates are a snap with iocage’s simplified container management syntax.

Powerful & Lightweight

iocage gives you the power to manage your jails efficiently while leaving only a small footprint on your system.

Safe & Secure

iocage builds on the trusted FreeBSD Jails system to add convenient ways to manage your cages without having to sacrifice the security you know and love.
